But Freud stuck to the idea of the mother’s body, specifically, the idea of the phallic mother that the child wants to believe in. All through the later psychoanalytic literature this idea occurs again and again in the fantasies of patients, and Robert Bak reaffirmed Freud’s basic idea in a most recent writing, in the same categorical terms.
