Most men are content to keep their meanings firmly in hand by refraining from extramarital infidelity; but one can narcissistically harbor his meanings even more by refraining from “heterosexual infidelity,” so to speak. From this point of view, when Freud talked about “the feminine side of his nature” he could just as well have been speaking from the strength of his ego rather than its weakness, from his own single-minded determination to engineer his own immortality.
