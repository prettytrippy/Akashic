The “Healthy-Minded” Argument There are “healthy-minded” persons who maintain that fear of death is not a natural thing for man, that we are not born with it. An increasing number of careful studies on how the actual fear of death develops in the child7 agree fairly well that the child has no knowledge of death until about the age of three to five.
