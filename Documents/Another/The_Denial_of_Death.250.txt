The orthodox psychoanalytic notion was that when the child witnessed sexual intercourse between the parents (the primal scene) it left him with a deep-seated trauma because he could not take part in it. Freud talked about the actual “stimulation of sexual excitement upon observation of parental coitus.”21 Put so bluntly the idea seems incredible enough, but we must remember that Freud prided himself above all on the discovery of infantile sexuality.
