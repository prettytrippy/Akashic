Brown warns us of the full radicalness of his reading of Freud by stressing that he resolutely follows Ferenczi’s insight that “Character- traits are, so to speak, secret psychoses.”20 This is shaking scientific truth, and we have also subscribed to it with Brown. If it has seemed hard for men to get agreement on such a truth during the age of Freud, one day it will be secure.
