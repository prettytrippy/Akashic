Brown claims that the “reunification of the ego and the body is not a dissolution but a strengthening of the human ego.”13 But this one phrase in passing rings hollow because it is truly empty chatter that avoids facing everything we know about the ego. To talk about a “new man” whose ego merges wholly with his body is to talk about a subhuman creature, not a superhuman one.
