According to his biographer Jones, Freud was subject to periodic anxiety attacks in which the anxiety was localized as a real dread of dying and of traveling by rail.22 In his attacks of dread of dying he had images of himself dying and of farewell scenes.23 Now this is quite a different matter than compulsive, magical games with the idea of death.
