It creates the difference that becomes such a burden; it accents the smallness of oneself and the sticking-outness at the same time. This is natural guilt. The person experiences this as “unworthiness” or “badness” and dumb inner dissatisfaction.58 And the reason is realistic. Compared to the rest of nature man is not a very satisfactory creation.
