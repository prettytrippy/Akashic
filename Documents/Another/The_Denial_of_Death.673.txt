Developments Beyond Freud Today we do not accept uncritically all of Freud’s arguments on group dynamics or consider them necessarily complete. One of the weaknesses of Freud’s theory was that he was too fond of his own phylogenetic myth of the “primal horde,” Freud’s attempt to reconstruct the earliest beginnings of society, when proto- men—like baboons—lived under the tyrannical rule of a dominant male.
